# MindBlock - ADHD-Friendly Time Blocking SaaS

## Vision
MindBlock is a time blocking productivity app specifically designed for ADHD brains. Unlike generic productivity tools, MindBlock is built with ADHD-specific workflows and features that help users manage time, reduce decision fatigue, and maintain focus.

## Core Differentiator
While competitors like Toggl, Clockify, Notion, and Fantastical offer generic time blocking, **MindBlock is the first time blocking app optimized for ADHD**. We understand:
- Shorter, frequent time blocks work better than long ones
- Transition time and break reminders are essential
- Visual feedback and quick wins provide dopamine hits
- Flexibility to reschedule mid-block prevents overwhelm
- Simplified UI reduces cognitive load

## Target Market
- People with ADHD who struggle with time management
- Neurodivergent professionals and students
- Anyone who finds traditional productivity apps too rigid
- High willingness to pay for solutions that actually work

## Current Product
- Web-based time blocking app with user authentication
- Kanban board for task organization
- Notes and day planner features
- Clean, simple interface designed with ADHD users in mind

## Future Roadmap (SaaS)
- Mobile apps (iOS/Android) - essential for on-the-go blocking
- Smart timer with visual progress indicators
- Adaptive break recommendations based on focus patterns
- Integration with calendar apps
- Freemium model with premium analytics
- ADHD community features (shared templates, strategies)

## Monetization
- Free tier: Basic time blocking (3 blocks/day)
- Pro: Unlimited blocks, analytics, integrations ($9.99/month)
- Team: Collaboration features ($19.99/month)

## Why This Works
The ADHD productivity tool market is growing but underserved. Users will pay premium prices for solutions actually built for their brain, not generic tools with marketing claims.
