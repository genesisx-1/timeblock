WHAT IT DOES

A normal todo list tells you what exists. Time blocking shows when it can happen. Block Day puts your hours, meetings, tasks, and notes in one place so your plan is visible.

01

DRAG TIME BLOCKS

Create blocks across a 24-hour timeline and label exactly what each part of the day is for.

02

ADD MEETINGS

Drop meetings into the calendar box and see them mixed into the day agenda with your blocks.

03

TRACK TASKS

Keep daily tasks beside your schedule and watch progress move as you check things off.

04

CUSTOM BOXES

Add or remove your own labeled note boxes for reminders, routines, calls, wins, or anything else.

HOW TO USE IT

The flow is simple: pick a date, block the hours, add the meetings, then write the notes you need for that day.

PLAN
